MOSS Radio

My first Ruby On Rails application, working with an awesome team of 3 others for our Le Wagon bootcamp final project.

The prototype/idea of MOSS is a community radio service using AJAX and minimal front-end styling for clean navigation and minimal page reloads. AJAX (Asynchronous JavaScript and XML) allows the central window to load content dynamically without requiring a full page reload to give a smooth user experience.


General Desgin

As an example of other design work with this pitch deck, I specialise in creating clean and minimal designs that effectively showcase a brand's identity. My approach involves taking the existing brand elements and exploring creative ways to present them in a unique and visually appealing way.

Through careful consideration of typography, colour, layout, and other design elements, I strive to create designs that are not only visually engaging but also functional and user-friendly. By maintaining a focus on simplicity and clarity, I ensure that the brand message is conveyed effectively and consistently across all design materials.
